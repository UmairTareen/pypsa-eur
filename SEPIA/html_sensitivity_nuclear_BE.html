Sensitivity Scenario on Nuclear Expansion

This sensitivity scenario is used to analyze the impact of nuclear investment costs on optimization results. The sensitivity analysis is based on the sufficiency scenario considering different cost assumptions used in available scenarios in literature. All the other parameters, consrtaints, demands follow the typical sufficiency scenario, only changes have been made in nuclear investment costs.

The main cost assumptions for nuclear in this sensitivity scenario include:

  • 8594 EUR/kW (Default PyPSA-EUR nuclear investment costs retrieved from Link ))
  • 7000 EUR/kW (FPS value used in "Investments needed to achieve climate neutrality in Belgium"
  • 6000 EUR/kW (World nuclear organization value used in Link ))
  • 4500 EUR/kW (Energyville value used in PATHS2050 scenarios for small modular reactors Link ))
